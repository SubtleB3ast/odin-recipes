Bruschetta

Toasted goodness of French bread, with the zip of tomatoes and cheese!

Ingredients:

  • French roll
  • diced tomatoes
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Brush both sides of bread with olive oil. Toast the bread for 10 minutes, flipping after 5 minutes
  2. Mix tomatoes and cheese, along with a drizzle of olive oil, into a mixing bowl.
  3. Spoon the tomato mix onto the toasted French roll, and enjoy!
