Chocolate Cake

Cake so moist, it melts in your mouth!

Ingredients:

  • milk
  • eggs
  • butter
  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • cream
  • vanilla extract
  • baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Coat two 8-inch pans with butter.
  2. Whisk the cocoa powder, vanilla extract, flour, eggs and milk, into mixing bowl.
  3. Pour in the mix into the 2 pans, filling evenly in both.
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes, testing with a toothpick to check for moistness.
  5. Let cool, the nspread on the cream. Serve and enjoy!
