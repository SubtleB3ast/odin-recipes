Spaghetti

Spaghetti so delicious, it tastes straight from Italy!

Ingredients:

  • Choice of pasta noodles
  • Jar of marinara sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Brown the meat while the water is heating up
  3. Once the water is boiling, add in the noodles for 10-minutes
  4. Add in the sauce to the meat once browned
  5. When noodles are soft, drain them into a collander
  6. Add noodles to bowl, cover with sauce, enjoy!
